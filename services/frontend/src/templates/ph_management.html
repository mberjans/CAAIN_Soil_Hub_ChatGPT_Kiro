Acidic Soil (pH < 6.0)
  • Aluminum toxicity risk
  • Poor phosphorus availability
  • Reduced microbial activity

Alkaline Soil (pH > 7.5)
  • Iron deficiency (chlorosis)
  • Zinc deficiency
  • Phosphorus fixation

Application Best Practices
  • Apply lime when soil moisture is adequate but not waterlogged
  • Incorporate lime within 2 weeks for optimal effectiveness
  • Avoid application before heavy rain forecast
  • Consider split applications for rates above 4 tons/acre
  • Retest soil pH 12-18 months after application
