USDA Hardiness Zones

Based on average annual minimum winter temperatures, these zones help determine which plants can survive winter in your area.

  • Zone 1: Below -50°F
  • Zone 3: -40°F to -30°F
  • Zone 5: -20°F to -10°F
  • Zone 7: 0°F to 10°F
  • Zone 9: 20°F to 30°F
  • Zone 11: Above 40°F
Köppen Climate Classification

Based on temperature and precipitation patterns, providing comprehensive climate understanding.

  • A: Tropical climates
  • B: Arid climates
  • C: Temperate climates
  • D: Continental climates
  • E: Polar climates
